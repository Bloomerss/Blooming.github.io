Air Pollution

SOLUTION SUMMARY

Students in Apec Schools are not aware of the dangers of Air Pollution so we thought of a solution to inform them and help them about decreasing the rate of air pollution.

OUR SOLUTION

We made our solution so that we could help the people about stopping air pollution. We also showed facts about air pollution. Our main focus was to put the solutions about decreasing it. We feel that Air Pollution is a problem that needs to be lessened and we feel that our solution can help in deacresing it.

1. It contains articles that have a lot of information about air pollution.
2. Consists of the causes and effects of air pollution.
3. Has solutions about air pollution.
4. It also has ways on how to protect youself whenever air pollution occurs.
