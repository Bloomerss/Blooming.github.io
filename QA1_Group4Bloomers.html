What is Air Pollution?

Air Pollution is the presence of substances in the air that has harmful effects

We always see air pollution outside and we want to reduce it because it can be really dangerous

OBJECTIVES:

At the end of our presentation, we are aiming to:

1. Let them know what air pollution is
2. For them to know how they should protect themselves when air pollution occurs
3. To be informed that air pollution is a problem that needs to be solved

SOLUTION:

Animation & Game

Articles and Factual Informations.

1. How air pollution is destroying our health.

ADVANTAGES:

1. It is informative and have information that can help you from air pollution.

2. It can gain attention from the viewers because it will be fun to watch.

3. It can bring the topic to life.

BENEFITS:

1. Can help the viewer stop and reduce the air pollution in their location.

2. Can be help them teach other people about air pollution.

3. It can inform them on how to keep themselves safe whenever air pollution happens.

Air Pollution Tips:

1. Repair the vehicles that put out bad smoke

2. Participate in community program about eliminating air pollution

3. Reduce dependance on fossil fuels for energy and transportation
